\section{Theoretical Approach}\label{sec:theo}
\subsection{ARMA Model}
The general scheme for the ARMA model is given by

\begin{equation}
  z_{t+1}=\sum_{s=0}^{l_1}a_sz_{t-s}+\sum_{s=0}^{l_2}b_s\xi_{t-s}
\end{equation}

    &y_t=H_tx_t+\zeta_t

\end{equation}
